Fitting method

Iterative non-linear least squares, using nlinfit in Matlab. 
Starting guess was log10(moment) - 14 (where the -14 was empirical) and the presumed fc assuming Brune stress drop of 5MPa. 
Fixed the moments relatively using moment magnitude of largest event, M4.53. 
Errors from Matlab nlparci, 95% confidence on the fit. Linear +/- error on fc and log10(Mo).

File Baltay_fit_lin performed on the linear spectra; Baltay_fit_log on the log spectra.
