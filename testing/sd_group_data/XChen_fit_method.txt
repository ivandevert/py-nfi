1. Used nonlinear solver in matlab to fit brune model
2. randomly resample residual to estimate corner frequency and moment error
    Mo_med and Mo_error come from 100 bootstrap resampling of the residual
    fc_med and fc_error come from 100 bootstrap resampling of the residual
    Mo_org and fc_org come from the original spectral fitting.
    the error is estimated from 95 quantile range. 
3. field description: 
    islog - 0 used linear spectral file, 1 used the ones with "log"
    id - event id
    mag - event magnitude
    Mo_org, Mo_med, Mo_error - Moment
    fc_org, fc_med, fc_error - corner frequency
